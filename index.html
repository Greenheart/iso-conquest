Iso Conquest

Welcome commander! In this minimalistic, turn based strategy game, your task is to seize control over the battlefield.

Conquer the areas of your opponent while saving your own. If you want to win, make sure you have the highest score in the end!

Strategy Tips

  1. Be aware of your opponent's options. Anticipating their next turns will help you make good decisions.
  2. Try to limit your opponent to a smaller part of the battlefield. If they can't make any further moves, you'll automatically win.
  3. Try to force your opponent to make bad moves: For example when they need to sacrifice a bonus area to avoid running out of moves, they could also lose the surrounding areas when it's your turn.
